App Ideas

UI design, concept

The Street History App is an educational tool to help visitors navigate and explore an unknown city. The Photo Translation App helps translate foreign signs, packages, etc. into English (or the language of your choice).

Street History App — Places of interests are marked on the map which users can tap on to see the overview. Tapping on that brings up background information culled from Wikipedia. Diamond Head photo by Daniel Ramirez.
Photo Translation App — A simple app that translates foreign text into your native language. Aim the phone camera at the object and select the from/to languages.
