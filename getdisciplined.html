GetDisciplined

Sole Developer and Designer

Android, Design, UI, UX, Java, XML, concept

GetDisciplined was one of the first apps I designed and developed myself. I did this project, not only to get some app design and development experience, but to solve a personal problem I have - wasting time on the smartphone.

Hours of your day can go to browsing Twitter, checking Facebook, playing on Snapchat or checking your Instagram notifications to see if your crush liked or commented on your photo. GetDisciplined is an android app that can block any selected app for up to 24 hours. When you try to open it, it'll will be blocked.

This project was one of my first major projects.

Here is the github link. Unfortunately Android constantly keeps changing their APIs and has deprecated core functionality I use to run this app. I open-sourced it so you can fork it and use the code in your own project if you wish.
