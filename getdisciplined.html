FAQ

Q: How does this app work?
It’s simple. Your smartphone can be a distraction while you need to get work done. You open the app and drag the scrubber (red dot) around the arc to set an amount of time in hours and minutes of how long you want the apps to be blocked for. Then, you hit the list button on the bottom-left and check which apps you want blocked. Finally, hit the start button on the bottom-right to block the apps for your selected amount of time!
Q: Will I be able to access my blocked apps if I turn my phone on and off?
No. You will only when the time runs out! We are smarter than that!
Q: Oh no! I blocked the apps, but I really want to use them now! How do I disable GetDisciplined?
Unfortunately, you can’t. The purpose of this app is to block distracting apps to help you be productive and focused. I feel for you though. It is really hard not to check Instagram or Facebook for over 5 minutes.
Q: GetDisciplined isn’t blocking my apps! Why!?
Oh that’s weird. If this is true, please email us at GetDisciplinedApp@gmail.com. We will fix it immediately! We want you to stay productive!
Q: Can you add a new feature that I thought of? I think it would make the app better!
We would love to! Please write a brief description for us on what it is, how it would improve the app, and why other users find it beneficial, and email us at GetDisciplinedApp@gmail.com.
Q: This is awesome, dude! But I have an iPhone! Is it coming to iOS soon?
Hopefully! Been really busy with college and work, but I will see if I can make an iOS version. My goal is the help you guys stay focused and GetDisciplined! ;)
